Core features

Everything an AI trader needs.

From strategy selection to live execution — Aegis handles the full lifecycle of every agent.

⚡

Multi-agent orchestration

Launch dozens of agents simultaneously — locally or on remote VMs. Each agent runs isolated with its own config, strategy, and position.

🧠

LLM strategy engine

Agents study historical market data and let your chosen LLM select or generate the optimal strategy. Re-evaluates on your schedule.

📊

Pre-built strategy library

Start immediately with momentum, mean reversion, funding rate arb, statistical arb, and volatility strategies — all battle-tested.

🔒

Risk-first architecture

Per-agent position limits, drawdown stops, and kill switches. The hub maintains authority — no agent can exceed its mandate.

🌐

Multi-market support

Trade perpetuals on Hyperliquid and dYdX, exploit arb across chains, or play prediction markets on Polymarket — all from one hub.

📡

Real-time monitoring

Live P&L, position snapshots, trade logs, and LLM reasoning — streamed to your dashboard, Telegram bot, or CLI in real time.

How it works

Configure. Deploy. Profit.

Three steps from intent to live trading agent.

1

Configure your agent

Set your token pair, market type, LLM provider, risk limits, and any custom strategy overrides. Takes under two minutes.

2

Deploy anywhere

Launch the agent locally or push it to a remote VM. Aegis hub tracks it, maintains heartbeats, and persists all state to the DB.

3

Monitor & iterate

Watch trades in real time. Agents auto-rebalance on your schedule. Pause, reconfigure, or scale any agent with a single command.
